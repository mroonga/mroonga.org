7. Community¶

There are some places for sharing Groonga and Mroonga information. We welcome you to join our community.

7.1. Mailing List¶

There are mailing lists for discussion about Groonga and Mroonga.

For English speakers

groonga-talk@lists.sourceforge.net

For Japanese speakers

groonga-dev@lists.osdn.me

7.2. Chat room¶

There are chat rooms for discussion about Groonga and Mroonga.

For English speakers

groonga/en chat room on Gitter

For Japanese speakers

groonga/ja chat room on Gitter

7.3. Twitter¶

@groonga tweets Groonga and Mroonga related information.

Please follow the account to get the latest Groonga and Mroonga related information!

7.4. Facebook¶

Mroonga page on Facebook shares Mroonga related information.

Please like the page to get the latest Mroonga related information!
